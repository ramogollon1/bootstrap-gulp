Privately owned corporation

specializing in sourcing & supplying commodities

Founded in 2007 under the laws of the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, Tracome Group Corp. (& quot; Tracome & quot;) is an independent commodity trading group and a commercial finance advisory firm. The company began operating actively in 2013. In 2014, we established the entity that is a subsidiary in Panama City, Panama known as Tracome Commodities Corp (TCC) for the future company. Treatments with CBT have the flexibility to obtain tax advantages and sources of funding.

Tracome's experience is to identify middle market producers with a history or capacity for export and import, mainly within the food industry in the United States, Latin America, Caribbean and Europe. Trace acts as an intermediary (broker or dealer ), Supply and delivery products worldwide. Also, it will take the position of the physical matter for the specific transaction. We provide full commercial finance advice to clients, from structuring and raising capital to executing and monitoring inventories and transactions. We will also manage and store the material inventory of the material to secure assets.
